Why this exists

A place to slow down

Contemplator is not a feed. Nothing here refreshes, and nothing is optimized to keep you scrolling. It holds a small number of ideas that have outlasted their authors, and asks you to spend real time with one at a time.

Read a line. Let the dot fill. Notice what rises before you move to the next one. That pause is the entire point — the thinking is yours to do, not ours to supply.
